Layout tips

Lead-in line — Put a phrase in brackets to show it on a smaller line above the main name:

  • [the princess] Luma Jaguar
  • [the countess] Ruby

Line break — Use | to force a new line:

  • Anna Nymph | Essie Hex → two lines
  • First Line | and | Third Line → three lines
